Professional Journey

Every stage of my career has contributed to my growth as an Intellectual Property Professional. Looking back, I see not just different roles, but meaningful experiences that have shaped my perspective, strengthened my expertise, and reinforced the importance of Continuous Learning.

From studying law to practising Intellectual Property across different organisations and jurisdictions, each chapter has broadened my understanding of innovation, brand protection, and the evolving role of Intellectual Property in today's global economy.

This journey continues today, driven by curiosity, Professional Development, and a genuine passion for learning.

⚖️ Building the Foundation

Chapter 1 | 2005 - 2015 | India

School of Excellence - The Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University | Practice at the Madras High Court

My professional journey began with studying law and gaining early exposure to legal practice. These formative years developed my analytical thinking, attention to detail, and appreciation for the role of law in society.

They also laid the foundation for my growing interest in Intellectual Property and the protection of innovation.

🌏 A New Beginning

Chapter 2 | 2018 - 2020 | Singapore

Trademark Secretary | Mirandah Asia, Singapore

Moving to Singapore marked an important turning point in my career.

Working in an international Intellectual Property environment introduced me to trademark practice, portfolio administration, and cross-border collaboration. This experience broadened my perspective and strengthened my interest in trademarks and global brand protection.

📈 Growing Through Experience

Chapter 3 | 2020 - 2023 | Singapore

Trademark Executive | Mirandah Asia, Singapore

As my responsibilities expanded, I became more deeply involved in trademark prosecution, portfolio management, and international filings.

Every matter presented opportunities to learn, strengthening both my technical knowledge and appreciation for the strategic role that Intellectual Property plays in protecting businesses and innovation.

🤝 Broadening My Perspective

Chapter 4 | 2023 - January 2026 | Singapore

IP Manager | Biro Oktroi Roosseno, Singapore

Joining Biro Oktroi Roosseno allowed me to broaden my exposure to regional and international Intellectual Property practice.

Working with colleagues and associates across different jurisdictions reinforced the importance of collaboration, Knowledge Sharing, and understanding diverse legal systems in today's interconnected world.

🌍 Continuing the Journey

Chapter 5 | January 2026 - Present | Singapore

Senior Manager - Global IP | Biro Oktroi Roosseno, Singapore

Today, I continue to deepen my understanding of Intellectual Property while supporting international trademark practice and global collaboration.

Beyond my professional responsibilities, I remain committed to Continuous Learning through international conferences, professional organisations, educational resources, and meaningful discussions with practitioners from around the world. Every new experience contributes to my ongoing Professional Development.

Lessons Along the Way

Rather than measuring success by job titles alone, I believe professional growth is defined by the willingness to learn, adapt, and continuously improve.

Each role has strengthened not only my technical knowledge but also my appreciation for collaboration, integrity, and lifelong learning.

The journey continues with the same curiosity that inspired me to enter the legal profession many years ago.

Looking Ahead

Intellectual Property continues to evolve alongside innovation, Artificial Intelligence, and emerging technologies.

As this landscape changes, I hope to continue learning, sharing knowledge, and contributing positively to the global Intellectual Property community.

This website represents that journey: a place to document experiences, reflect on lessons learned, and encourage greater awareness of Intellectual Property through education and Continuous Learning.

"The beautiful thing about learning is that no one can take it away from you."

- B.B. King
