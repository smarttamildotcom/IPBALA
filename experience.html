Professional Journey

Every stage of my career has contributed to my growth as an Intellectual Property Professional. Looking back, I see not just different roles, but meaningful experiences that have shaped my perspective, strengthened my expertise, and reinforced the importance of Continuous Learning.

From studying law to practising Intellectual Property across different organisations and jurisdictions, each chapter has broadened my understanding of innovation, brand protection, and the evolving role of Intellectual Property in today's global economy.

This journey continues today, driven by curiosity, Professional Development, and a genuine passion for learning.

⚖️ Building the Foundation

Chapter 1 | 2005 - 2015 | India

A foundation shaped by legal study, specialised postgraduate learning, and early court practice.

  • Bachelor of Law (LL.B.) - School of Excellence, The Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University
  • Post Graduate Diploma in Intellectual Property Laws (Completed in 2010)
  • Practice at the Madras High Court

My professional journey began with studying law at the School of Excellence - The Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University, where I built a strong foundation in legal principles, analytical thinking, and problem-solving.

Driven by a growing interest in Intellectual Property and the protection of innovation, I pursued a Post Graduate Diploma in Intellectual Property Laws, which I successfully completed in 2010. This specialised programme broadened my understanding of trademarks, patents, copyrights, and other areas of Intellectual Property, complementing my legal education and strengthening my interest in this field.

Alongside my academic journey, I gained practical legal experience through my practice at the Madras High Court. This early exposure to legal practice enhanced my analytical skills, attention to detail, and appreciation for the legal profession.

Together, my legal education, specialised postgraduate studies in Intellectual Property, and practical legal experience laid the foundation for my future career as an Intellectual Property Professional.

🌏 A New Beginning

Chapter 2 | 2018 - 2020 | Singapore

Trademark Secretary | Mirandah Asia, Singapore

Relocating to Singapore marked a significant milestone in my professional journey, offering the opportunity to work in one of Asia's leading Intellectual Property environments. It was a new chapter that exposed me to international clients, diverse industries, and the complexities of protecting Intellectual Property across multiple jurisdictions.

As a Trademark Secretary, I gained valuable experience supporting trademark prosecution, portfolio administration, and the coordination of filing and prosecution activities across different countries. Working closely with legal professionals, clients, and foreign associates strengthened my understanding of international trademark procedures and the importance of accuracy, organisation, and effective communication.

This experience broadened my perspective on Intellectual Property practice and sparked a lasting interest in trademarks, global brand protection, and the strategic role Intellectual Property plays in supporting businesses and innovation worldwide. It also provided a strong foundation for the responsibilities I would take on in the years ahead.

📈 Growing Through Experience

Chapter 3 | 2020 - 2023 | Singapore

Trademark Executive | Mirandah Asia, Singapore

Stepping into the role of Trademark Executive marked an important stage in my professional development, allowing me to take on greater responsibilities and become more actively involved in managing Intellectual Property matters.

During this period, I worked on a wide range of trademark matters, including prosecution, portfolio management, international filings, and supporting brand protection strategies across multiple jurisdictions. Each matter presented unique challenges that enhanced my technical knowledge, strengthened my analytical and problem-solving abilities, and deepened my appreciation for the commercial value of Intellectual Property.

Working with clients from diverse industries further broadened my understanding of how trademarks contribute to business growth, innovation, and long-term brand value. This phase of my career reinforced the importance of continuous learning, adaptability, and attention to detail, while preparing me for leadership opportunities and more complex responsibilities in the field of Intellectual Property.

🤝 Broadening My Perspective

Chapter 4 | 2023 - January 2026 | Singapore

IP Manager | Biro Oktroi Roosseno, Singapore

Joining Biro Oktroi Roosseno marked an exciting new chapter in my professional journey, providing the opportunity to broaden my exposure to regional and international Intellectual Property practice. Working within a firm with a strong presence across Asia enabled me to gain a deeper appreciation of the diverse legal frameworks, business environments, and cultural perspectives that shape Intellectual Property protection across different jurisdictions.

As an IP Manager, I became involved in a wider range of Intellectual Property matters, supporting trademark portfolio management, international filings, and cross-border collaboration with clients, colleagues, and foreign associates. These experiences strengthened my ability to manage complex matters, coordinate across multiple jurisdictions, and deliver practical solutions while maintaining accuracy and attention to detail.

This stage of my career reinforced the importance of collaboration, knowledge sharing, and continuous learning. It also broadened my understanding of how Intellectual Property contributes to business strategy, innovation, and the protection of valuable brand assets in an increasingly interconnected global marketplace. The experience prepared me for greater leadership responsibilities and a broader international perspective.

🌍 Continuing the Journey

Chapter 5 | January 2026 - Present | Singapore

Senior Manager - Global IP | Biro Oktroi Roosseno, Singapore

Today, I continue to build upon the experiences gained throughout my professional journey while supporting international trademark practice and collaborating with clients, colleagues, and professionals across multiple jurisdictions. Every new matter presents an opportunity to deepen my understanding of Intellectual Property, strengthen professional relationships, and contribute to the protection of brands in an evolving global environment.

In addition to my professional responsibilities, I remain committed to continuous learning and professional development. Participating in international conferences, engaging with professional organisations, exploring emerging technologies, and exchanging knowledge with practitioners from around the world have become integral parts of my career. These experiences allow me to stay informed about developments in Intellectual Property and broaden my perspective on the challenges and opportunities facing the profession.

As the Intellectual Property landscape continues to evolve alongside technological advancement and global innovation, I believe that learning is a lifelong journey. I look forward to continuing to grow professionally, sharing knowledge with others, and contributing to the advancement of the Intellectual Property community while embracing new opportunities for collaboration and innovation.

Lessons Along the Way

Rather than measuring success by job titles alone, I believe professional growth is defined by the willingness to learn, adapt, and continuously improve.

Each role has strengthened not only my technical knowledge but also my appreciation for collaboration, integrity, and lifelong learning.

The journey continues with the same curiosity that inspired me to enter the legal profession many years ago.

Looking Ahead

Intellectual Property continues to evolve alongside innovation, Artificial Intelligence, and emerging technologies.

As this landscape changes, I hope to continue learning, sharing knowledge, and contributing positively to the global Intellectual Property community.

This website represents that journey: a place to document experiences, reflect on lessons learned, and encourage greater awareness of Intellectual Property through education and Continuous Learning.
