About

A concise profile of my learning-focused professional interests in intellectual property, with a particular focus on trademarks and knowledge sharing.

Professional Background

My professional interest is centred on trademarks and the important role they play in protecting brands, supporting innovation, and creating long-term business value. I enjoy learning about trademark law, brand protection strategies, portfolio management, and the evolving legal and commercial developments that shape intellectual property across different jurisdictions.

In addition to trademarks, I maintain a broader interest in other areas of intellectual property, innovation, and emerging technologies, recognising the increasing importance of intellectual property in today's global economy.

Professional Journey

Throughout my career, I have remained committed to continuous learning and professional development, with a particular focus on trademarks and international brand protection. I value opportunities to deepen my understanding through professional publications, conferences, discussions with practitioners, and developments in global intellectual property practice.
