About

A personal story of why intellectual property matters to me, how trademarks became a core professional interest, and how continuous learning shapes my journey.

PHMy Philosophy

"Intellectual property is the oil of the 21st century."

- Mark Getty

This idea resonates with me because intellectual property has become central to how innovation is created, protected, and sustained. In a knowledge-driven economy, intangible assets increasingly influence business resilience, long-term competitiveness, and societal progress.

IPWhy Intellectual Property?

Intellectual property brings together law, innovation, business, creativity, and technology in a uniquely meaningful way. It supports entrepreneurship, protects original ideas, encourages creative work, and contributes to sustainable economic growth across industries and geographies.

TMWhy Trademarks?

For me, trademarks are far more than legal registrations. They represent identity, reputation, trust, brand value, customer recognition, and long-term business growth. This connection between legal structure and strategic business impact is what makes trademarks especially fascinating and professionally meaningful.

PJProfessional Journey

My professional path has gradually strengthened my understanding of intellectual property and international trademark practice. Over time, practical responsibilities, professional reading, conferences, and discussions with practitioners have each contributed to a deeper and more balanced perspective.

Rather than viewing growth as a destination, I see it as an ongoing process shaped by reflection, discipline, and sustained curiosity.
