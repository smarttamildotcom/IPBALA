International IP Organisations

  • WIPO — World Intellectual Property Organization Global forum for IP services, policy, information, and cooperation.
  • INTA — International Trademark Association Global association supporting trademark owners and professionals.
  • TRIPS Agreement — WTO The Agreement on Trade-Related Aspects of Intellectual Property Rights.

National Patent Offices

  • USPTO — United States Patent and Trademark Office Official US federal agency for granting US patents and registering trademarks.
  • EPO — European Patent Office The European authority for examining and granting patents in Europe.
  • EUIPO — EU Intellectual Property Office EU agency managing trade marks and designs in the European Union.
  • IPO — Intellectual Property Office (UK) The official UK government body responsible for intellectual property rights.

AI & Technology Resources

  • WIPO — Technology & Innovation Support WIPO's resources on frontier technologies, AI, and IP at the intersection of innovation.
  • EPO — Artificial Intelligence & Patents EPO's dedicated resources on AI inventions, patentability, and AI-assisted tools.
